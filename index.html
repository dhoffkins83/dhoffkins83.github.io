Workforce Management & Queue Optimization

Problem

Queues and staffing didn’t match demand, creating avoidable backlog and inconsistent response times.

What I did

Tightened queue strategy and workforce planning, aligned capacity to volume, and created clearer queue health reporting.

Impact

More predictable support coverage, improved queue health, and fewer “surprise” escalations.

KPIs that Drive Behavior (Not Just Reporting)

Problem

Metrics existed, but they weren’t connected to coaching and decisions — so they didn’t change outcomes.

What I did

Refined KPIs, built dashboards, and tied weekly reviews + coaching to a small set of actionable indicators.

Impact

Clearer accountability and stronger performance rhythms; contributed to improved CSAT/NPS in blended teams.

Leadership, Coaching & Performance Uplift

Problem

Scaling teams needed consistent coaching and leadership structure across tiers.

What I did

Implemented structured coaching and mentorship, developed team leads, and reinforced performance expectations with data.

Impact

Improved agent performance and more consistent coaching across support tiers.

Incident Management & High-Priority Escalations

Problem

Urgent issues required a clear system for triage, ownership, and fast response — especially at scale.

What I did

Built and scaled an incident response motion, clarified escalation paths, and introduced consistent SOPs.

Impact

Faster urgent issue response and clearer ownership under pressure.
