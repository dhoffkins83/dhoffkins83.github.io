How I operate

My CX Ops playbook

This is the loop I run to turn “support feels chaotic” into “support feels predictable.”

1) Diagnose Queue health, volume patterns, SLA misses, top drivers
2) Align Staffing ↔ demand, routing rules, ownership, escalation paths
3) Instrument Dashboards + weekly cadence; make “good” visible
4) Coach Feedback loops that scale: managers, QA, enablement
